How to play: HUE Says? Game
Playing the game

To start the game click the "Start Game button". The button will now be un-clickable and the sequence will play. The HUE lights will change colour to show the sequence, the buttons will also change colour to show the sequence. Once the sequence is done, you will now be able to input the sequence. The lights will turn green until the sequence is done. If a wrong sequence is entered, the lights and background of the website will flash red, then the score will be reset.

High Score : HUE Says? Game
High Scores

High scores for this game are automatically saved in your browser. The "Session High Score" shows the highest score since the game has been running in your browser. The "All Time High Score" Shows the highest score that has been achieved by you from ALL of you sessions. We do not save any of your data, it is saved to your browser.

Lights : HUE Says?
Lights

The light integration in the game is explained on the left card. On the lights page, it shows how the HUE lights work and allow you to test them out. HUE lights use a number from 1 to 50000 to indicate colour, you can play with this by entering your own number here. You can also turn the lights on and off or set the lights to flash here.
